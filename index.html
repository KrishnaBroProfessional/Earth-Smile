Wake Up Call

What's Happening to Our Planet?

The Earth is changing — faster than ever before. Here is what our planet looks like through decades of data.

🌡️

Rising Temperatures

The last decade (2011–2020) was the warmest on record. Global average temperatures have already risen 1.1°C above pre-industrial levels.

🧊

Melting Ice Caps

Arctic sea ice is declining at 13% per decade. At current rates, we could see an ice-free Arctic summer by 2035.

🌊

Rising Sea Levels

Global sea levels are rising at 3.6 mm per year. Coastal cities face existential threats within this century.

Real-time: 50 years of global warming

NASA visualization: Earth's temperature anomalies (1880–2023)

1.5 °C Threshold
Critical Alert

The Point of No Return

Scientists warn that crossing 1.5°C of warming triggers irreversible feedback loops — amplifying themselves beyond human control.

  • Permafrost Thaw — releases stored carbon, dramatically accelerating warming
  • Amazon Dieback — the world's lungs stop absorbing CO₂
  • Ice-Albedo Feedback — less ice means less solar reflection means more heat
  • Coral Reef Collapse — marine biodiversity crashes irreversibly
We are currently at +1.48°C and rising

Our Action Plan

The Initiative

Earth Smile is more than awareness — it's a structured, community-driven plan to make Shantigram the model for a plastic-free India.

01

Community Awareness Drives

Door-to-door campaigns educating residents about single-use plastic alternatives and their environmental impact on Shantigram’s ecosystem.

Ongoing
02

Plastic Collection Points

Designated collection zones across Shantigram for segregated plastic waste, with verified disposal and recycling partnerships.

Phase 1
03

School Green Programs

Integrating environmental education into local schools with hands-on activities, eco-competitions, and sustainability pledges for students.

Active
04

Tree Plantation Drives

Planting native species across Shantigram to restore biodiversity, reduce urban heat islands, and offset local carbon emissions.

Phase 2
05

Local Business Partnerships

Partnering with vendors and businesses to transition to eco-friendly packaging and earn certified plastic-free outlet status.

Upcoming
06

Zero Waste Shantigram 2027

Our flagship goal: make Shantigram 100% plastic-free and establish it as a replicable model for communities across India.

Target: 2027
